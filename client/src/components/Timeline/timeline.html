10 SEPTEMBER 2019

Placement

The real ability of IIITL was proven in 2019-2020 placements where the highest salary package offered was up to 43 LPA, whereas the average salary package offered was 18.42 LPA. The lowest salary package offered was 10 LPA. This year there was a 100% placement rate, which is almost not possible in new institutes. Top recruiting companies here were prestigious companies like Amazon, Google, Microsoft, LinkedIn, Flipkart, Samsung, Paytm, TCL, Wipro and many more.
